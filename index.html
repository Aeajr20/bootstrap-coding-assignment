TIME TO GET SCHWIFTY!

Rick and Morty is a popular animated science fiction sitcom. It follows the misadventures of an eccentric, alcoholic scientist named Rick Sanchez and his good-hearted but easily influenced grandson Morty Smith, who split their time between domestic family life and interdimensional adventures.
